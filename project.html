Air Quality Monitoring System

Components Used:

  • MQ135 Gas Sensor
  • DHT11 Temperature & Humidity Sensor
  • OLED Display
  • Arduino Board
  • Breadboard & Jumper Wires

Working:

The system uses an MQ135 sensor to detect air quality by measuring gas levels, while the DHT11 sensor records temperature and humidity. An Arduino processes the sensor data and displays real-time results on an OLED screen, categorizing air quality as Good, Poor, Very Bad, or Toxic.
